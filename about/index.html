I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, currently a visiting researcher at Princeton University, advised by Prof. Pramod Viswanath. My research is on the foundational scientific and engineering topics that are crucial to blockchains being ubiquitous in society.

I received the Bachelor’s degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in ACM Honors Class, Zhiyuan College. In 2019, I was a research intern at UIUC advised by Prof. Bo Li. In SJTU, I worked on Automatic Speech Recognition and Machine Learning with Prof. Kai Yu and Prof. Yanmin Qian in SpeechLab.
