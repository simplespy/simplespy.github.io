Peiyao Sheng

I am a Researcher at Sentient. My research focuses on the foundational scientific and engineering topics that are crucial to blockchains and AI being ubiquitous in society. I am particularly interested in the security and governance of decentralized systems, and their interaction with AI agents.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Pramod Viswanath. Previously, I was a visiting researcher at Princeton University. During my Ph.D., I co-founded Witness Chain, a startup that aimed to unify the DePIN economy, exemplifying the practical application of my research. I have also interned at Chainlink Labs, advised by Dr. Dahlia Malkhi.
